# Hero Born: 3D Action Prototype

A tactical survival experience where movement and timing are everything. Evade a high-speed robot, gear up, and take your shot.

## Core Gameplay
* **Strategic Progression:** Player must collect a health pickup and arm themselves before engaging the enemy.
* **Smart AI (NavMesh):** The enemy uses pathfinding to chase the player, responding to their presence in real-time.
* **Limited Ammo Mechanics:** Players only have 3 bullets. Every shot counts.
* **Integrated HUD:** Real-time health and ammo tracking in a clean UI.

## Key Technical Features
* **Third-Person Controller:** Smooth movement and camera orientation.
* **Physics-Based Projectiles:** Bullets with collision detection and automatic cleanup.
* **State Management:** Winning/Losing conditions linked to player actions and enemy status.

## Tech Stack
* **Unity 6 / C#**
* **NavMesh AI**
* **Starter Assets Integration**
